STEP 3 - SET UP CRON-JOB (FREE)
================================================================================

Your endpoint only runs when someone visits the URL. To check for new
messages automatically, you need a free cron service:

  a) Go to https://cron-job.org
  b) Sign up for a free account
  c) Create a new cron job:
     - URL: https://messenger-responder.vercel.app/api/respond
     - Interval: Every 5 minutes
     - Save it

This will call your endpoint every 5 minutes, checking for new "hi" messages
and auto-replying "hello".

IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

  1. Cold starts: Every ~5-15 minutes of inactivity, Vercel clears the
     /tmp folder. Your app will need to log into Facebook again. This
     might trigger Facebook security checks. If you get banned, you'll
     need to run this on a VPS (like Railway, Render, or DigitalOcean).

  2. Your .env.local file on your computer is safe - it was never
     committed to GitHub thanks to .gitignore.

  3. If the endpoint fails with a Facebook login error, it means Meta
     detected the automated login. This is a known risk with unofficial
     Facebook automation.
